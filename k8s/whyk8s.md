# Why Kubernetes (K8s)?

Here are the key reasons and benefits of using Kubernetes:

## 1. Cloud Agnostic & Easy Migration
* **No Vendor Lock-in:** Kubernetes abstracts the underlying infrastructure, making your services cloud-agnostic.
* **Easy Migration:** Migrating applications between different cloud providers (e.g., from AWS to GCP or Azure) is simple and straightforward, without requiring code rewrites.

## 2. Infrastructure Flexibility
* **Hybrid & On-Premise Support:** You are not locked into any specific cloud provider. You can run the exact same Kubernetes configurations on-premise (bare metal or virtual machines) or on any public cloud.

## 3. Automated Operations & Maintenance (Self-Management)
Kubernetes takes over the operational overhead of managing your applications by providing:
* **Auto-Scaling / Scaling:** Dynamically adjusting the number of running instances based on resource demand or traffic.
* **Load Balancing:** Automatically distributing traffic across container instances to ensure high availability and stability.
* **Self-Healing:** Continuously monitoring container health and automatically restarting, replacing, or rescheduling containers that fail or become unresponsive.
